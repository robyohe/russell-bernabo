Gilded Surfaces

Frames intended to be beautifying companions to paintings, drawings, and prints can devolve into unsightly liabilities. The frame and artwork, once engaged in a meaningful dialogue, are no longer on speaking terms. Our specialization in gilded frames can help resolve this conflict with conservation treatments that are visually pleasing, wholly ethical, and reliably budget conscious.
